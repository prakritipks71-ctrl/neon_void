🕹️ FLIGHT CONTROLS

Use W A S D or Arrow Keys to navigate your ship in cosmic space. The ship moves instantly and stops precisely when keys are released.

Press Spacebar or Right-Click to trigger **Hyper-Dash**, rendering you invulnerable and pushing you forward at light speed.

⚔️ COMBAT PROTOCOLS

All weapons are auto-firing. Focus on dodging enemies and gathering floating **XP Crystals** (light green gems) and **Credit Cores** (yellow stars).

Levelling up pauses the game to allow you to adapt your ship. Combine physical laser weapons with gravity wells and orbital shields for optimal survival.

⚙️ MODULAR SHIP DESIGN

Harvest Credits from runs to spend in the **Ship Hangar** on permanent ship parts:

  • Hulls increase your max armor health and shield defense thresholds.
  • Shields govern recharge delays and add dynamic discharge loops (static shock when broken).
  • Engines increase movement impulse and reduce Hyper-Dash cooldowns.
  • Weapon Cores provide unique starting weapons.
